ABOUT TWEMI COLLEGE OF DISCIPLESHIP AND EVANGELISM

Founded in 2010, True Worshipers Evangelical Ministries International College of Discipleship and Evangelism exists to provide accessible, values‑driven higher education to students from all walks of life. We blend academic rigor with spiritual growth, preparing graduates for both career and purpose.

OUR ENHANCED MISSION

MISSION: To equip and empower believers worldwide through biblically grounded education, fostering deep discipleship, passionate evangelism, and transformative leadership. We are committed to raising a generation of servant-leaders who will impact their communities and the nations with the gospel of Jesus Christ, demonstrating excellence, integrity, and compassion in every sphere of life.

OUR ENHANCED VISION

VISION: To be a premier global center for discipleship and evangelism training, producing graduates who are spiritually mature, theologically sound, and practically skilled to advance God's kingdom. We envision a movement of believers equipped to make disciples of all nations, bringing revival and reformation through the power of the Holy Spirit, and establishing centers of faith that transform societies.
